Answer Engine Optimization for the AI-search era.

What it is

The next decade of "SEO" isn't ranking on Google — it's being mentioned by LLMs in their answers. AeoBox is the toolkit for that:

  1. Visibility scan — query the major answer engines with your customers' real questions, log when your brand shows up
  2. Citation tracking — which sources are the LLMs pulling from? (this is the new backlink graph)
  3. Content recommendations — what kinds of pages get cited? (it's not what SEO assumes)
  4. Competitor benchmark — same checks against your category

  • Cloudflare Workers (scheduled scans)

Why now

Search behavior is shifting fast — Gartner projects 25% of search will go through generative engines by 2026. Today's SEO playbooks weren't built for this. There's a window to build the canonical tool.
